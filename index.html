Asset management

ReLife was created at RTE for asset management based on reliability and renewal theory.

Open science

ReLife is open source and tries to gather both statistical knowledge and pratical examples to conduct appropriate risk analysis for asset management.

Easy to use

ReLife is coded in Python and was designed to be accessible for user from any programming background or experience level.
